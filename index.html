Quentin Tarantino FAQ

How old is Quentin?

61, he was born on March 27th, 1963

Where did Quentin grow up?

He was born in Knoxville, Tennessee, but moved to Torrance, California when he was four years old.

What did Tarantino do before writing and directing movies?

Quentin worked in a video store in California. At that time, he wrote the scripts for 'True Romance' and 'Natural Born Killers'.

Has Tarantino ever acted in his own movie?

Yes! He has appeared in 30 of his movies, such as 'Django Unchained' and 'Pulp Fiction'
