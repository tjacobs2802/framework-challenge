*******Questions and Answers About Quentin Tarantino*******

Q1. How old is quentin?
61, he was born on March 27th, 1963.

Q2. Where did Quentin grow up?
He was born in Knoxville, Tennessee, but moved to Torrance, California when he was four years old.

Q3. What was Quentins first big hit?
Tarantino's 1992 film 'Reservoir Dogs' put him on the map, as that was his first
critically acclaimed movie when that film appeared at the Sundance Film Festival.

Q4. What did Tarantino do before writing and directing movies?
Quentin worked in a video store in California. At that time, he wrote the scripts for 'True Romance' and 'Natural Born Killers'.

Q5. What is Tarantino's net worth?
His net worth is approximately $120 million.

Q6. Has Tarantino ever acted in his own movie?
Yes! He has appeared in 30 of his movies, such as 'Django Unchained' and 'Pulp Fiction'
